About country

Spain is a fascinating country with a rich history, diverse culture, and beautiful landscapes. Known for its delicious cuisine, vibrant cities, and stunning beaches, Spain has become a popular destination for both tourists and business travelers.

About accomodation

Spain is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world and offers a wide lodging range that expands constantly. According to DBK, a Spanish consulting firm, Spain has almost 17,000 hotels and around 1,755,000 beds.
